Who We Are

Since 1966, Animals in Distress has worked to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome thousands of animals across South Africa.

About Animals in Distress

Animals in Distress (AID) is a South African non-profit organisation that has been operating since 1966. The organisation provides essential veterinary services, rescues abandoned or abused animals, and works to improve animal welfare in underprivileged communities.

Mission Statement

To relieve the suffering of animals by providing accessible veterinary care, rescuing abused and neglected animals, and promoting responsible pet ownership through education and community outreach.

Vision Statement

To create a compassionate society where every animal is respected, cared for, and given the opportunity to live a healthy and happy life.

Our Vision

A society where all animals are respected, cared for, and free from neglect or abuse.
